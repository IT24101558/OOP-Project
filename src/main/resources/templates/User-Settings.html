Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my workouts?
You can track your workouts by going to the Exercises section, selecting your workout plan, and clicking on "Start Workout". The app will guide you through your exercises and record your progress.
How do I change my subscription plan?
You can change your subscription plan in the Billing section of your Settings. Choose the plan you want and follow the prompts to update your subscription.
Can I sync with other fitness devices?
Yes! FitConnect can sync with most popular fitness trackers and smartwatches. Go to the Settings page and look for "Connected Devices" to set up your integrations.
